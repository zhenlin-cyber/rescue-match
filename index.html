How Rescue Match Works

01

Enter Your Birthday

Share your birth month and day. We search Bay Area shelters for rescue animals who arrived — or were found — on that exact date, creating a connection that goes beyond the algorithm.

02

Choose Your Energy

Are you a couch cuddler, a weekend explorer, or a daily adventurer? Great companions understand each other’s pace — your energy shapes who you’ll meet.

03

Follow Your Constellation

Choose the Felis Celestial (cats) or the Canis Celestial (dogs). Your constellation guides you to a species and a soul waiting in a shelter nearby.

04

Meet Your Match

Browse real rescue animals from Bay Area shelters. Every profile links directly to the rescue team — one email away from beginning your story together.

Rescue Match is a free tool connecting you to real animals at real shelters. We do not charge adoption fees — all inquiries go directly to the rescue organizations who care for these animals.
